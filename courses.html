Bachelor of Science in Information Technology

The BSIT is a four-year degree program in Saint Jude College Manila that deals with the processing and distribution of data with emphasis on its application on businesses.

By enrolling in this program you will learn how to design, and maintain computer programs; check them for errors; determine appropriate solutions to fix problems, and rewrite codes to accomodate necessary changes.
